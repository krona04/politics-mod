## ğŸ“– About the Mod

**PoliticsMod** brings a comprehensive political and economic simulation layer to Minecraft. Unlike simple claiming mods, PoliticsMod focuses on the structure of a nation: from founding a capital to managing individual cities, population, and economy.

Whether you are building a small kingdom or a massive empire on a Roleplay server, this mod provides the tools to secure your land, manage your citizens, and simulate a living country.

## ğŸš€ How to Start

1.  **Switch to Creative Mode:** Open your inventory and find the custom tab **"Politics Craft"**.
2.  **Found a Country:** Place the **Founding Stone**. Right-click it and enter your desired Country Name.
3.  **Build a City:** Travel to a new location, place a **City Stone**, and give your city a name.
4.  **Claim Land:** Open the dashboard (press `K` or Right-Click the stone) and go to the "Map" tab to claim chunks.
5.  **Grow Population:** Place **Residential Buildings** near houses with beds to simulate population growth.
